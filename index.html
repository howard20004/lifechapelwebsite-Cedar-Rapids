About Life Chapel Cedar Rapids

Life Chapel Cedar Rapids is a vibrant, Christ-centered church dedicated to helping people encounter God, grow in faith, and build meaningful relationships. We believe church is more than a Sunday service—it’s a family where lives are transformed through love, worship, and the Word of God.

At Life Chapel, everyone is welcome—no matter your background, your story, or where you are in your journey. Our heart is to create a place where hope is restored, purpose is discovered, and faith comes alive.

Through powerful worship, practical teaching, and a strong sense of community, we are committed to raising disciples who impact their families, their city, and the world for Christ.

Whether you’re searching for a church home or just exploring faith, there is a place for you here.

Come as you are—and experience life, love, and purpose in Christ.
